DEFORESTATION AND EFFECTS OF DEFORESTATION

WHAT IS DEFORESTATION??

DEFORESTATION means clearing of forests and using that land for other purposes

Trees in the forest are cut for some of the purposes mentioned below -

  • Procuring land for cultivation.
  • Building houses and factories.
  • Making furniture or using wood as fuel.

    • EFFECTS OF DEFORESTATION

    After knowing what deforestation is let's now know in what ways does it effect our enviroment.

  • Increase in temprature
  • Increases pollution level on earth
  • Increases the level of carbon dioxide
  • Ground water level also gets lowered
  • Disturbs balance in nature
  • Changes the soil properties
  • Fewer trees result in more soil erosion
  • Gradually the fertile land gets converted into deserts by the process called DESERTIFICATION.
  • Changes the nutrient content , texture etc.. of soil
  • Decreases the water holding capacity of the soil

    Some facts
